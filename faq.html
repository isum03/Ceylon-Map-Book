Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is Ceylon Map Book?

It is a website designed in order to guide local and international visitors about various destinations enriched with not only beauty but also Cultural and religious values all around Sri Lanka.

How can I find the places which I want to travel?

Website has six main topics namely ,

  • Northern Side
  • Historical Landmarks
  • Up Country
  • Down South
  • Commercial Capital
  • East Coast

All the places are categorized under these main topics. You can easily find your desired places by navigating through these main topics. Each place has a brief description to get to know about the place and to have a better understanding before the travel.

Can I make hotel reservations / request tour packages through the site?

No. You cannot reserve hotels or obtain tour packages through this website. You may contact nearby hotels / travel agencies for such concerns.This website was developed purely with the aim of providing a better guidance and to help you to make your travel more comfortable and memorable.

Do I need to pay in order to find a place?

No. The whole website is accessible free of charge.

How can I clarify a doubt about a place?

You may reach us through ceylonmapbook@gmail.com
